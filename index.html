About ERA

Get ready for an impactful two-day camp with IEDC AWH, the college chapter of the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre. This camp offers ITalks, hands-on workshops, and a bootcamp for aspiring entrepreneurs, providing students the opportunity to learn from industry experts, network with successful entrepreneurs, and gain hands-on experience. Open to all AWHEC students, regardless of background or major, this camp aims to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and support to succeed in entrepreneurship. Register now for this exciting and transformative experience.
